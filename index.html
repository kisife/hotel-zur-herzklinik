About Us

A Peaceful Retreat in the Heart of Bad Oeynhausen

Located in the beautiful spa town of Bad Oeynhausen, Hotel zur Herzklinik offers a welcoming and comfortable stay for guests visiting the Heart Clinic or exploring the wellness attractions of the region. Our property features charming garden views, a shared kitchen for your convenience, and a relaxing terrace where you can unwind.

Just a 5-minute walk from the renowned GOP Varieté Theater and within close proximity to the Bali Therme spa and picturesque Kurpark, our guesthouse is perfectly positioned for both medical visitors and leisure travelers seeking tranquility and comfort.
